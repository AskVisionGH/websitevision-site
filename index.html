Features

Everything you'd do on-chain, in one sentence.

No more ten-tab trading. No more signing things you don't understand. Just ask.

01

Trade in plain English

"Buy $50 of WIF at market." Vision picks the best route across every DEX, simulates the trade, and asks before it signs.

  • Jupiter & 1inch aggregation
  • Slippage & impact preview
  • Confirmation before any signature
02

Bridge without thinking

Move assets between chains without learning a new UI. Vision finds the cheapest, fastest route and handles the rest.

  • Auto-routing across major bridges
  • Fee & ETA transparent upfront
  • One confirm, both sides settled
03

Live market context

Prices, flows, narratives, launches. Ask what's happening on pump.fun or what whales are buying today.

  • Real-time feeds from major chains
  • On-chain wallet tracking
  • Narrative & sentiment signals

Safe by default

Every on-chain action is simulated and summarised in English before you approve. No mystery signatures, ever.

  • Human-readable transaction preview
  • Contract & token safety checks
  • Nothing moves without explicit approval
